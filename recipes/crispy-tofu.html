Crispy Tofu

Description

fast, easy and yummy crispy tofu cubes that goes with every side dish

Ingridients

  • tofu
  • 1/3 cup of cornflor
  • 1 spoon of olive oil

    • Souce

  • 2 spoons of natural penut butter
  • 3 spoons of soy souce
  • 1 table spoon of maypel syrop
  • 1 spoon of rice vinigart
  • 1/3 cup of water

Steps

  1. cut the tofu into cubes
  2. dip the tofu cubes in the cornflor

    3. Souce

  3. mix all the ingridients in a cup or a jar
  4. fry the floered tofu cubes on a heated pan with hot oil
  5. add the souce mix to the pan and fry
