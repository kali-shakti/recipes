Tomato and Zucchini pie

Tomato zucchini pie

Description

gluten free, vegan tasty pie that can turn into a delicious breakfast.

Ingridients

  • 3 medium zucchinies
  • 2 cups of shery tomatos
  • 1 cup of red lentil flour
  • 1 cup of thick oats
  • 1/2 a cup of almond flour
  • 1 spoon of apple vinigart
  • 1 cup of unsweeten soy milk
  • 1 spoon of olive oil
  • 1 table spoon of soda
  • 1 shredded onion
  • 1/2 a table spoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 a table spoon of oregano
  • 1/2 a table spoon of curcum
  • 1/2 a table spoon of rosmary
  • 1/2 a table spoon of dried onion

Steps

  1. slice ahe tomatos ans zucchinies
  2. put in oben for 20 minutes with olive oil and rozmary for tomatos
  3. fry the onion
  4. in small bowl mix soy milk and soda
  5. in a large bowl mix all the dry Ingridients
  6. add to the big bowl the milk and soda
  7. add the veggies from the oben to the large bowl and mix
  8. put in oben dish and decorate with some of the veggies
  9. bake on 200 for 45 minutes
