Veggies and Rice

Description

one dish quick and full of nutritions meal

Ingridients

  • 1 cup of whole rice
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlics
  • 1 yam
  • 1 cup of green long beens
  • 2 cups of soy beans
  • 1 table spoon baharat
  • 1 table spoon of cummin
  • 1 table spoon of dry persly
  • 1/2 table spoon of cinnamon
  • 5 spoons of soy souce
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of boiling water

Steps

  1. cut all the veggies
  2. mix all the ingridients in a bowl
  3. move to a oben dish
  4. bake in the oben on 200 for 50 minutes
